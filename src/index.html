Features

Security

With GreenVault, your investments are secured through decentralized protocols, reducing the risk of centralized vulnerabilities. Our robust encryption and decentralized architecture provide an extra layer of security, safeguarding your assets against potential threats

Accessibility

Unlike traditional investment platforms, GreenVault is open to everyone, regardless of geographical location or financial background. Our decentralized model eliminates barriers to entry, allowing anyone to participate in the growing world of DeFi and crypto hedging

Diversification

GreenVault enables investors to diversify their portfolio efficiently. Through our platform, you can access a wide range of crypto assets and hedging strategies, spreading risk and maximizing potential returns in various market conditions

Efficiency

By leveraging decentralized technologies, GreenVault eliminates the need for lengthy approval processes and bureaucratic hurdles. Transactions are executed swiftly and efficiently, enabling you to capitalize on market opportunities in real-time

Transparency in all transactions has special value for us. Our investment platform is completely transparent in its operations and any user's code verification, which is achieved through the use of automated smart contracts on the Ethereum and Ton platforms. In addition, all of our smart contracts have been audited. The results of the audits and the code of the smart contracts can be found here.
